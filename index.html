Your solution to disaster preparation

Keeping track of impending emergency warnings and obtaining information pertaining to safe shelters can be difficult due to the many different resources offered by individual states and counties. Also, it can often be more favorable to seek shelter in nearby buildings and houses than official shelters in some cases. Neighbors and people close by may be in a better position to help people in crises but may be unaware of who requires their help too. Our project aims to demonstrate a solution which will provide instructions on how to survive in various disasters, information on nearby shelters, and allow people to request for aid to their current location or volunteer to help somebody in need.

Request Help

Request for help to the public in our Request Help page!

Fill out the required fields on the form, including your name, address, contact, date and time requiring help. To inform potential volunteers, also write down some details about your situation. Submit to post your request on our website listings.

Volunteer Help

Volunteer to help the public in our Request Help page!

Input your address or ZIP code, and see the list of closest requests for help. Click on a request in the list for more information. If you wish to volunteer, fill out the required fields on the form, including your name and contact. Submit to send your information to the requester and wait for contact.
